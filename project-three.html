UI/UX 2024

Journey Magazine

Overview

Journey Magazine is a conceptual travel publication focused on premium, visually-led storytelling. Guided by the tagline “Find Your Soul,” the magazine promotes reflective, meaningful travel. This issue features South Korea, with a focus on Gangwon Province—an understated, nature-rich destination.

My Role

I was responsible for the full design process, including concept development, layout design, typography selection, and image curation.

Year

2024

Problem / Goal

The goal was to design a travel magazine that feels modern, polished, and visually engaging while maintaining strong readability.


A key challenge was balancing large, immersive imagery with structured editorial content, ensuring the layouts felt clean and intentional rather than cluttered.


The design needed to guide the reader naturally through the content while reinforcing a premium, aspirational brand feel.
