Web Design

JourneyJanes Landing Page

Overview

JourneyJanes is a boutique travel platform designed for the modern solo female traveler who seeks adventure without the isolation. The platform bridges the gap between independent exploration and group safety, offering curated "Travel Solo Together" experiences that foster lifelong connections among women.

My Role

UI Designer

Tools

Figma

Problem / Goal

Problem: Research shows that while women are the fastest-growing segment of solo travelers, many hesitate due to safety concerns or the social "friction" of exploring alone.

Goal: Design a landing page that feels both adventurous and secure. The UI emphasizes "Connect" and "Memories" to prove that "solo" doesn't have to mean "lonely."

Images

Outcome

JourneyJanes landing page reframes solo travel as a shared, enriching experience. With “Connect” and “Memories” at the forefront, it builds trust and community, encouraging users to explore confidently and engage with trips.
