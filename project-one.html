UI/UX

Skill Up Mobile Learning App

Overview

Skill Up is a mobile micro-learning concept for telecom field technicians, designed to deliver quick, practical learning within the flow of their workday.

My Role

UIUX Designer

Tools

Figma

ChatGPT & Gemini

Claude AI

Problem / Goal

Traditional learning is too long and desktop-focused for field technicians working under time pressure. The goal was to create a fast, mobile-first experience that supports quick access, short sessions, and easy resumption of learning.

Process
01
Research Identified user needs, behaviours, and constraints within a fast-paced, mobile field environment.
02
Define Established core goals and principles to guide a low-friction, micro-learning experience.
03
Design Created wireframes and high-fidelity UI focused on clarity, speed, and ease of use.
04
Prototype Built an interactive prototype to simulate real-world usage and task flow.
05
Refine Improved the experience through scenario-based testing, reducing friction and simplifying key actions.

Outcome

A streamlined learning experience that enables fast access, minimal friction, and consistent engagement within a technician’s daily workflow.
